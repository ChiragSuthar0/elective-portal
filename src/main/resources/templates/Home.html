Elective Choice Filling

Please read and follow the instructions properly

  • Login with the Username and Password provided to you
  • Your Password will be a combination of <Your Roll Number>_<Phone Number>. Once logged in you can change your password
  • Follow the link to choice-filling given in the navigation bar to fill you choices for the electives
  • You can have 5 different choices for the electives. Starting from 1st Preference all the way to 5th.
  • Once you have filled your choices you will not have the option to alter the choices so please fill them carefully.
  • You can check your filled choices in view my choices from this link.

Some Additional Instructions

For Students above semester 3

  • Before filling out your choices, upload your previous semester's Marksheet / MOOC Certificate using the Upload option you see in he navigation bar
  • If already have not choosen SLS dept once you must register for an SLS offered course.
